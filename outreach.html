Community Outreach

ICB Wayland welcomes visitors, school groups, and interfaith dialogue across eastern Massachusetts.

Our Commitment

Bridging Communities

The Islamic Center of Boston in Wayland is committed to building bridges with our neighbors of all faiths and backgrounds. We offer visits, speaker programs, and active participation in local interfaith events throughout eastern Massachusetts. All are welcome.

Sunday or Friday Visit

Observe congregational prayers and get a guided introduction to the mosque, Islamic practice, and our community. Perfect for interfaith groups and curious neighbors.

Sunday: 12:30 – 1:30 PM (Sep – June)
Friday: 12:45 PM – 2:00 PM

School & College Groups

We host weekday educational visits for school and college groups. Sessions last up to 2 hours and cover Islamic history, beliefs, and practice, with time for Q&A.

Weekdays: 9:00 AM – 4:00 PM
Up to 2 hours per session

Visitor Etiquette

Modest dress is appreciated. Shoes are removed before entering the prayer hall. Men and women pray in separate sections. Questions are always welcome.
