Join Our Community

Become a Member

Membership supports ICB's operations and programs while connecting you more deeply to the community.

Individual Membership

For individual Muslims who want to be part of the ICB family, support the mosque, and participate in community decision-making.

Family Membership

Covers your entire household and includes Sunday school enrollment benefits. The most popular option for families with children.

Why Membership Matters

Membership funds directly support daily operations, facility maintenance, staff, and community programs that benefit everyone who walks through our doors.
