Programs & Initiatives

From education and youth development to community service and interfaith engagement — there's a place for everyone at ICB.

Sunday Islamic School

A full K–10 curriculum covering Islamic Arabic and Religion, designed to develop confident, compassionate American Muslims. Classes run September through early June.

Learn More →

Youth Group

The ICB Youth Group offers social, educational, and service activities for young Muslims. A space to connect, grow, and give back to the community.

Learn More →

Community Outreach

We welcome school groups, interfaith visitors, and speaker requests. ICB actively participates in local interfaith events across eastern Massachusetts.

Charity & Service

Annual fundraisers, humanitarian walks, and ongoing charitable initiatives support causes locally and globally. Join our First Annual Humanitarian Walk on May 9.

College Merit Scholarship

ICB Wayland awards merit-based scholarships to outstanding Muslim students pursuing higher education. Applications open annually.
