Prayer Times

Daily Iqamah schedule and Friday Jumu'ah information for ICB Wayland.

Daily

Iqamah Times

Note: These are Iqamah (congregational prayer start) times. Adhan is called approximately 10–15 minutes prior. Listen to Adhan ↗
Prayer Iqamah Notes
Fajr 5:30 AM Dawn prayer
Zuhr 1:30 PM Midday prayer
Asr 5:45 PM Afternoon prayer
Maghrib 7:40 PM Sunset prayer
Isha 9:00 PM Night prayer

* Times may vary slightly by season. Check the website for the most current schedule.

Jumu'ah — Friday Prayer

Khutbah begins: 1:00 PM

Iqamah: 1:30 PM

All are welcome to attend Friday prayer. The khutbah (sermon) is delivered in English.

Featured Speaker (Apr 24): Dr. Mohamed Lazzouni

Sunday School Prayer

During the Sunday school session (September through early June), Zuhr prayer is held congregationally at 12:30 PM for students, parents, and teachers.

Visitor Etiquette

Modest dress is required. Women are asked to cover their hair. Shoes are removed before entering the prayer hall. Men and women pray in separate areas.
