Who We Are

A Center for Faith, Community, and Connection

ICB Wayland has been a home for Muslims in eastern Massachusetts since 1982. We gather for daily prayers, run a Sunday school for children in grades K–10, and work closely with our neighbors through outreach and community programs.

Whether you are a longtime member, a new family in the area, or simply curious about Islam — you are welcome here.
