About the Youth Group

Where Young Muslims Belong

The ICB Wayland Youth Group is a space for young Muslims to build friendships, deepen their faith, and make a positive impact. We organize social events, educational discussions, community service projects, and more — all in a welcoming, fun environment.

The Youth Group is designed and maintained by ICB Wayland Youth Group members themselves — giving young people real ownership over their community space.

Social Events

Regular gatherings, game nights, outings, and community dinners that help youth build lasting friendships within the ICB community.

Islamic Learning

Halaqas, discussions, and guest speaker sessions that help young Muslims explore their faith, ask questions, and grow in knowledge.

Community Service

Youth-led service projects and charity initiatives that embody the Islamic spirit of giving back, locally and beyond.

Leadership

Youth take on leadership roles in organizing events and managing the group — developing real-world skills alongside Islamic character.
