Our Space

Services & Facilities

ICB Wayland offers prayer spaces, social halls, and classrooms available for community use.

Main Prayer Hall

A large, carpeted prayer space used for all five daily prayers, Friday Jumu'ah, Eid prayers, and special congregational programs. Separate sections for men and women.

Social Hall

A spacious multi-purpose hall available for community dinners, events, lectures, and celebrations. Equipped with tables, chairs, and a kitchen area.

Classrooms

Dedicated classroom spaces used by the Sunday Islamic School for K–10 instruction, small group learning, and youth programming.

Wudu Facilities

Dedicated ablution (wudu) facilities for both men and women, conveniently located near the prayer hall entrance.

Parking

On-site parking is available for worshippers and visitors. Additional street parking is accessible on nearby roads during high-attendance events.

Facility Booking

Community members and organizations may request use of ICB's social hall and classrooms for approved events. Bookings are subject to availability and ICB guidelines.
